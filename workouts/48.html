Full Body Circuit

@tcrosleyfit

I’ve been doing mostly full body workouts for the past year and allowing myself more days to rest and recover. I know everyone is different, but I feel more satisfied training this way. I sweat more and feel amazing (and whooped🥵) after.. not to mention, the results are showing! Sometimes a simple change up in your routine can make a worlds of difference mentally and physically🙌🏼 don’t be afraid to play around with your fitness routine and switch up! Here’s the FULL BODY circuit: do 4 rounds with 1 minute rest in between each round ✖️Sumo Squat Pulses x30 sec ✖️Goblet Hold Reverse Lunges x30 sec each side ✖️DB Press ✖️Lunge Hold w/ Oblique twist (twist towards front leg).. this can also be done with a DB!
